Dismaland. 8/21/2015-9/27/2015. Weston-super-Mare, Somerset, England.

Dismaland was an exhibition funded by Banksy, and contained works from him and 58 other
artists. Thought to be inspired by artist Jeff Gillette’s Dismayland paintings, Dismaland
was meant to be a “bemusement park”, described as “a sinister twist on Disneyland” and a
“family theme park unsuitable for children.”
