HW Week 3

I wholeheartedly believe that the internet has transcended any other invention to the
point where it requires classification of its own. It is a vital part of human life
and needs to be treated as one of the necessities of life instead of being regulated
or shunned out of more classical modes in which we interact. Web-integration is not a
luxury or bonus anymore. Web needs to be free and equal. It is the most precious form
of communication that allows for synchronous and asynchonous information interchange,
a incredible flexibility that suits it for all types of communities and industries.
