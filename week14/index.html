Pop Culture Character Quiz

~ "Which Star Wars Character are you?" type of quiz.
~ Answers will have a certain amount of points attached to them.
~ Points will be tallied up as the user progresses, and the result will be splashed on the screen at the end.

~ Each press of the "Next" button will replace the text of the question element and the 4 answer elements.
~ On the last question, the "Next" button turns into "Submit".
